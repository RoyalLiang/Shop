Company Introduction

Qingdao Oradar crafts co.,ltd ,is a new trading company,located in Qingdao city,whose factroy ,cao county yuguang crafts co.,ltd covers an area of 4,000 square meters and is located in the beautiful hometown of peony - Heze City, Shandong Province.


Our factory founded in 1997,who has its own office building , show rooms, production workshops, coating and drying rooms and other facilities. With over 120 working staff members, our turnover is around RMB 5million.


All products are exported to America, mid-east countries and European countries. We are a professional manufacturer of handmade arts and crafts. Our main products are home storage items,including plastic weaving storage baskets,laundry baskets, fabric storage baskets and wooden decor items and other accessoryhome decro goods. 


We have the ability to meet the requirements of different customers to make the products according to the customers' designs . At present, we invest more in the R&D to develop all kinds of new products and they are very popular among old and new customers. With strong technical advantage, rich resources, fast delivery, reliable quality control and competitive prices, we gain a high reputation among our customers. We would like to build long-term and stable relationship with our customer and achieve great success in the arts and crafts industry.
